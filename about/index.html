ABOUT

The Students' Technical Council is a council of all the technical groups present in the institute namely SDS, MDG, ArIES, MaRS, Design Studio, ShARE, Tinkering Lab, EDC, Physics and Astronomy, TIDES and SAE/FSAE. Until the year 2017, the council used to go by the banner of Hobbies Club comprising of just 8 groups. Since then we have grown to 11 groups in number.
